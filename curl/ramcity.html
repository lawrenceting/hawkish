Trim Enabled

Supports TRIM on your Mac out of the box, on all TRIM-featured OSX versions, up to and including OSX Yosemite.

What is TRIM?

SSDs are faster than traditional hard disk drives, but they can't keep up their quick speed forever. All SSDs have built-in Garbage Collection routines to keep the SSD fast and responsive. TRIM works by complimenting garbage collection and allows the early removal of previously deleted data, reduces write amplification, and improves overall endurance. SSD wrk for Mac is the only 3rd party SSD that supports TRIM for Mac natively, and it works in all versions of OSX that support TRIM, including Yosemite and higher. No driver, KEXT disabling software, or TRIM Enabler is needed!

If you want to learn more about TRIM support for SSDs on OSX Yosemite, then read this article on the RamCity blog.
